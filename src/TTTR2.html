Tic Tac Toe is a game where each player strives to play three of their own marks horizontally in a row, vertically in a column, or diagonally across the board.

This version works much the same although the computer opponent is practically a master, yet one who is sensitive to unkind words. If it is taunted, it may become distracted by any offense taken while making its play.

Likewise, be sure to steel yourself for any sneers thrown in your direction. Good Luck!

  • Hints:
  • Computer may not play well if intimidated.
  • Intimidated players can't taunt during their turn.
